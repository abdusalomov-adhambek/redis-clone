# Redis Clone — 18-ETAP Nazariyasi (RESP Encoder)

# Encoder

Encoder:

Go ma'lumotlarini
RESP formatiga o'tkazuvchi kod

hisoblanadi.

---

# RESP Encoder Maqsadi

RESP Encoder:

server ichidagi javoblarni
RESP protocol formatiga
aylantiradi

---

Misol:

OK

↓

+OK\r\n

---

# RESP Response

RESP Response:

server clientga yuboradigan
RESP formatdagi javob

hisoblanadi.

---

# Serialization

Serialization:

Go object yoki qiymatni
network orqali yuborish uchun
matn formatiga o'tkazish

jarayoni.

---

Misol:

5

↓

:5\r\n

---

# Simple String

Simple String:

oddiy muvaffaqiyatli javoblar

uchun ishlatiladi.

---

RESP format:

+<message>\r\n

---

Misol:

+OK\r\n

---

+PONG\r\n

---

# EncodeSimpleString

Vazifasi:

matnni Simple String
formatiga o'tkazish

---

Misol:

EncodeSimpleString("OK")

↓

+OK\r\n

---

# Error Response

Error Response:

xatolik haqida
ma'lumot qaytaradi

---

RESP format:

-<message>\r\n

---

Misol:

-ERR invalid command\r\n

---

# EncodeError

Vazifasi:

errorlarni RESP Error
formatiga o'tkazish

---

Misol:

EncodeError("ERR invalid command")

↓

-ERR invalid command\r\n

---

# Bulk String

Bulk String:

matnli qiymatlarni
qaytarish uchun ishlatiladi

---

RESP format:

$<length>\r\n
<data>\r\n

---

Misol:

$5
Adham

---

# Length Prefix

Length Prefix:

ma'lumot uzunligini
oldindan ko'rsatish

degani.

---

Misol:

Adham

↓

5 ta harf

---

Shuning uchun:

$5
Adham

ko'rinishida yuboriladi.

---

# EncodeBulkString

Vazifasi:

string qiymatlarni
RESP Bulk Stringga
aylantirish

---

Misol:

EncodeBulkString("Adham")

↓

$5
Adham

---

# Null Response

Null Response:

qiymat topilmaganda
qaytariladi

---

RESP format:

$-1\r\n

---

Misol:

GET unknown

↓

$-1\r\n

---

# EncodeNull

Vazifasi:

mavjud bo'lmagan qiymatlarni
RESP Null formatida qaytarish

---

# Integer Response

Integer Response:

sonli natijalarni
qaytarish uchun ishlatiladi

---

RESP format:

:<number>\r\n

---

Misol:

:1

---

:100

---

# EncodeInteger

Vazifasi:

sonlarni RESP Integer
formatiga o'tkazish

---

Misol:

EncodeInteger(5)

↓

:5\r\n

---

# Array Response

Array:

bir nechta qiymatlarni
qaytarish uchun ishlatiladi

---

RESP format:

*<count>\r\n

---

Misol:

*2
$4
name
$3
age

---

# EncodeArray

Vazifasi:

slice ichidagi qiymatlarni
RESP Array formatiga o'tkazish

---

Misol:

[]string{
    "name",
    "age",
}

↓

*2
$4
name
$3
age

---

# CRLF

CRLF:

\r\n

belgilari.

---

RESP protocolidagi har bir qator:

CRLF

bilan tugaydi.

---

# Protocol Compliance

Protocol Compliance:

RESP qoidalariga
to'liq amal qilish

degani.

---

Agar:

\r\n

to'g'ri ishlatilmasa:

redis-cli

javobni noto'g'ri o'qishi mumkin.

---

# Centralized Encoding

Centralized Encoding:

barcha javoblarni
bitta encode package orqali
boshqarish

degani.

---

Misol:

Handler
 ↓
Encode
 ↓
Client

---

Bu kodni:

tozaroq
o'qilishi osonroq
kengaytirilishi qulayroq

qiladi.
